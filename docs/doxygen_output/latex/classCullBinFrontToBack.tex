\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A specific kind of \doxylink{classCullBin}{Cull\+Bin} that sorts geometry in order from nearest to furthest based on the center of its bounding volume.

This is useful for rendering opaque geometry, taking optimal advantage of a hierarchical Z-\/buffer.
