\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A constraint between two rigid bodies, each with a pivot point. The pivot points are described in the body\textquotesingle{}s local space. The constraint limits movement of the two rigid bodies in such a way that the pivot points match in global space. The spherical constraint can be seen as a "{}ball and socket"{} joint.
