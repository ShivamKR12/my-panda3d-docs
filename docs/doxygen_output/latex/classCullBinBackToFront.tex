\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A specific kind of \doxylink{classCullBin}{Cull\+Bin} that sorts geometry in order from furthest to nearest based on the center of its bounding volume. This is primarily intended for rendering transparent and semi-\/transparent geometry that must be sorted from back to front.
