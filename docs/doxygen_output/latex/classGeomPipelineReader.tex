\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Encapsulates the data from a \doxylink{classGeom}{Geom}, pre-\/fetched for one stage of the pipeline.

Does not hold a reference to the \doxylink{classGeom}{Geom}. The caller must ensure that the \doxylink{classGeom}{Geom} persists for at least the lifetime of the \doxylink{classGeomPipelineReader}{Geom\+Pipeline\+Reader}.
