Set position of the "{}ear"{} that picks up 3d sounds NOW LISTEN UP!!! THIS IS IMPORTANT! Both Panda3D and Open\+AL use a right handed coordinate system. But there is a major difference! In Panda3D the Y-\/\+Axis is going into the Screen and the Z-\/\+Axis is going up. In Open\+AL the Y-\/\+Axis is going up and the Z-\/\+Axis is coming out of the screen. The solution is simple, we just flip the Y and Z axis and negate the Z, as we move coordinates from Panda to Open\+AL and back. What does did mean to average Panda user? Nothing, they shouldn\textquotesingle{}t notice anyway. But if you decide to do any 3D audio work in here you have to keep it in mind. I told you, so you can\textquotesingle{}t say I didn\textquotesingle{}t.
