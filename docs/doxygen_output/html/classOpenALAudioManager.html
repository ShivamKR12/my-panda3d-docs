Set position of the "ear" that picks up 3d sounds NOW LISTEN UP!!! THIS IS IMPORTANT! Both Panda3D and OpenAL use a right handed coordinate system. But there is a major difference! In Panda3D the Y-Axis is going into the Screen and the Z-Axis is going up. In OpenAL the Y-Axis is going up and the Z-Axis is coming out of the screen. The solution is simple, we just flip the Y and Z axis and negate the Z, as we move coordinates from Panda to OpenAL and back. What does did mean to average Panda user? Nothing, they shouldn't notice anyway. But if you decide to do any 3D audio work in here you have to keep it in mind. I told you, so you can't say I didn't.
